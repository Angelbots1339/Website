Our sponsors are the people who allow us to participate in FRC regionals, and World Championships, the people we respect for their time and who believe we can win in our FIRST robotic competitions. All of Angelbotics thanks them for their help in getting us further every year, and we also thank them for continuing to help us in the future. We will always accept anyone who wishes to donate or sponsor us in our pursuit to better our team and win first place. In donating you will receive a tax deduction and may receive recognition in our team's pit or on our robot.
